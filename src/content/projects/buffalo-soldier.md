## Overview

Exploration of tactility and material (substrate) to sonically communicate historical facts and speculation.

## Conceptual Framework

An investigation of the co-commodification of Black bodies and nature by government and military industrial complex from the inception of the "Buffalo Soldier" to present.

## Narrative

Speculative narrative of a buffalo soldier encountering an American Buffalo (Bison) and having a shared memory of harvest, slaughter, and rebirth.

## Personal Connection

A connection with the researcher's lived experience and his father's fascination with the history and depictions of Buffalo Soldiers and Black Cowboys.
